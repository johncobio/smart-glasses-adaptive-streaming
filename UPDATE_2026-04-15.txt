    Instead, battery update (glasses_battery_update) is used as the trigger.

========================================
INTERPRETATION
========================================

The 61% reduction in ingest latency confirms that MentraCloud's relay was
the dominant source of delay — not the hardware or network.

The remaining ~2820ms startup delay is firmware-level behavior:
  - Mentra glasses buffer ~3s of video locally before pushing RTMP
  - This is documented in Mentra's platform as an intentional prebuffer
  - Cannot be reduced without modifying glasses firmware

The MentraCloud relay added on top of this prebuffer via:
  - sleep 2 stabilization wait (~2000ms)
  - FFmpeg -analyzeduration probe (~500-1000ms)
  Both are now eliminated by the bypass.
